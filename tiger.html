Tiger Details

Type : Carnivorous

The Tiger is a large cat of the genus Panthera native to Africa and India. It has a muscular, deep-chested body, short, rounded head, round ears, and a hairy tuft at the end of its tail. It is sexually dimorphic; adult male Tigers are larger than females and have a prominent mane


Scientific name: Panthera tigris

HHeight: 70 – 120 cm (Adult, At Shoulder)

Speed: 80 km/h (Maximum, In Short Bursts)

Trophic level: Carnivorous Encyclopedia of Life

Lifespan: 10 – 15 years (Adult, In the wild), 8 – 10 years (Adult, In the wild)
