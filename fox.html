Fox Details

Type : Carnivorous

The red fox is the largest of the true foxes and one of the most widely distributed members of the order Carnivora, being present across the entire Northern Hemisphere including most of North America, Europe and Asia, plus parts of North Africa.


Scientific Name: Vulpes vulpes

Trophic level: Omnivorous

Eats: Arctic fox, Leporids, Squirrels, Raccoons, Hamsters, Opossums, Gopher, Gerbils, Arvicolinae, Murids

Trophic level: Carnivorous Encyclopedia of Life

Lifespan: 3 – 4 years (In the wild)

Height: 35 – 50 cm (Adult, At Shoulder)
